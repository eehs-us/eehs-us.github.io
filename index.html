Who We Are

EEHS is a division of Consultancy Group Co. (CGC) providing expertise in Environmental Engineering and Health Sciences. Established in 1996, CGC is a civil engineering firm specializing in geotechnical/geo-environmental engineering, soils and materials testing, quality control, structures, roads, dams, and research and technology transfer. CGC operates internationally and has carried out major projects in the Middle East, Africa and South East Asia. EEHS expands the consulting role of CGC into the environmental and health domains. The division includes a cadre of experts in environmental engineering, soils engineering, and public health with excellent track records of executed projects.

Our Mission

At EEHS, we strive to:

  • Offer a wide array of environmental, health and safety services and technologies to our clients.
  • Diversify our services to fit any business, governmental, or nonprofit sectors.
  • Supply our clients with cost-effective solutions.
  • Recognize that each project –whether large or small – is of utmost importance to our clients.
